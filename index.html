About Shan Badminton School

Our Mission

Our mission is to elevate badminton performance through elite coaching, modern training methods, and a commitment to excellence, preparing players for competitive and professional success.

  • Programs for ages 5+, beginner to advanced
  • Strength & conditioning tailored to badminton
  • Personalised skill & competition plans

Head Coach — Shan

With extensive national-level playing experience and a proven track record of coaching successful athletes, our Head Coach leads the training program with expertise, passion, and precision. He specialises in advanced technique development, tactical awareness, and performance conditioning—ensuring every player receives world-class guidance tailored to their goals.

Assistant Coaches

Our Assistant Coaches are trained professionals who bring strong competitive experience and coaching knowledge to the court. Skilled in advanced technique development, strength and conditioning, and tactical training, they ensure that every athlete receives structured, goal-focused support. Their professionalism, teamwork, and passion for coaching strengthen the school’s mission of producing disciplined and high-performing badminton players.
